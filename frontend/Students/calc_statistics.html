Population vs Sample

Which Standard Deviation should you use?

  • Population (σ): Use when you have data for every member of the group (e.g., grades of every student in class). Divide by N.
  • Sample (s): Use when your data represents a subset of a larger group (e.g., surveying 50 people out of 1000). Divide by N-1.
