Understanding BMR

What: Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR) is the number of calories your body burns at complete rest just to keep you alive (breathing, digestion).

Why: Knowing your BMR is the first step to calculating your TDEE (Total Daily Energy Expenditure), which guides weight loss or gain plans.

How: Uses the Mifflin-St Jeor equation, considered the most accurate standard formula.
