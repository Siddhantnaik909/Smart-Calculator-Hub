About Body Fat

What: This tool estimates the percentage of your total weight that is adipose tissue (fat).

Why: Body fat % is a superior indicator of fitness compared to BMI because it distinguishes between muscle and fat mass.

How: It uses the US Navy Method, calculating body density based on the relationship between your height and body circumferences.
