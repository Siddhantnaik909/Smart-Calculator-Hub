About the Due Date

What: Estimates the date your baby is likely to be born.

Why: Helps in tracking fetal development milestones and scheduling prenatal visits.

How: Uses Naegele's Rule: Adds 280 days (40 weeks) to the first day of your Last Menstrual Period (LMP).
